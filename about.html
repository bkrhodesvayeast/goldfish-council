Who we are and what we breed.

About The Goldfish Council

We're a small-scale goldfish breeding operation focused on fancy varieties — orandas, ranchus, lionheads, and more. Every fish featured on the Goldfish Videos page is bred and raised on-site, with video previews so buyers can see how each fish actually swims before making a decision.

Our Fish

We specialize in fancy goldfish breeds with an emphasis on healthy body shape and finnage. Each fish is quarantined and monitored before being listed. See the Goldfish Videos page for current fish with video previews, and reach out if you'd like more details on a specific one.

Contact

Interested in a fish you saw on the Goldfish Videos page? Reach out with the goldfish's name and SKU or listing date, and we'll follow up with availability and pricing.
